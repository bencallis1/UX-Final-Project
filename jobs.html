FRONT-END DEVELOPER

   

We are looking for an experienced and passionate front-end developer who will be able to balance creativity and functionality and will also be able to bring our desings to life! The applicant should be fluent in Greek & English, have a team spirit, have a passion for detail, be willing to learn new things and be self-motivated.

Responsibilities:

- Collaborating closely with designers and developers to deliver pixel perfect websites
- Developing and maintaining websites / newsletters / eshops / wordpress themes
- Managing your own workflow on multiple projects
- Participates in code reviews as needed and as assigned by their respective project team leads and department leadership. 
- Being extremely attentive to detail and architectural approach
- Keep a clean & well structured code
- Flexible thinking & problem solving
- Build websites with high performance with consistent viewing across various OS platforms and browsers

Requirements:

- Preferably university-level degree in Computer Science or Electronic Engineering.
- Minimum of two years experience in similar position.
- Responsive cross platform development
- Extensive knowledge of HTML5 & CSS3
- LESS or SASS, Compass
- PHP & MySQL
- JavaScript / jQuery / Ajax
- UX and interactivity
- Understand the importance and use of Semantics & SEO
- Wordpress
- Website optimization
- Experience in using Photoshop
- Being familiar with Back-end systems
- Familiarity with W3C recommendations
- Willingness to adapt to the company coding / formatting standard if needed.

Bonus skills:

- e-Commerce experience, such as Magento
- Parallax
- GIT
- JS Frameworks
- Canvas
- Using 3rd party API’s

If we have drawn your attention, you can draw ours by providing either:

- Your CV
- Your portfolio
- A link to your personal blog – if any
- A Github account or any profile with open source contributions proving your familiarity with the requirements.
- Any other account that might be beneficial in proving your autonomy (bitbucket / codepen / coderwall etc.)
