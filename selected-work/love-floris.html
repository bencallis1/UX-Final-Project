Love Floris

In this particular project we were assigned with designing a calendar for Love Floris, a New York based company which organizes exclusively journeys to Greece. Aiming at pointing out the journeys’ destination, we dedicated every month of 2015 to an Olympian god.

Visually dismantling each deity, we used colors transpiring the Greek character and the particularity of the journeys, accentuating each god’s main trait.

By using minimal but also intense elements, we enable the recipients to discover a personality in each card and to travel through Love Floris in the magical world of Greek mythology.
