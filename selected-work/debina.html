Debina

A semi-sparkling white wine that is named after the famous local white grape variety of Zitsa, “Debina”.

The creative objective was to highlight the name of this local variety through the commanding typography presented in shades of light blue that exude freshness, happiness.

The silver bezel around the embossed label, surrounds its typeface, creating a meaningful connection with both the bubbles of the product and the concept of the cycle, the Greek sun light and friendship.
