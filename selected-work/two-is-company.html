Two Is Company

“Two is company” is a design and production company of luxury furniture, light fittings, carpets and decorative objects. Also, it undertakes architectural and interior design projects of buildings, orientating towards modernistic inspiration and international identity.
Our goal was the creation of a complete corporate website, focusing on

easy navigation, quick finding of “Two Is Company” products and compatibility with all screen resolutions and all devices. The website design was based on the Greek identity transpired by the company’s projects and was realized through typography and geometric details. At the same time it toys with a color palette fully harmonized with that of the company’s creations.
